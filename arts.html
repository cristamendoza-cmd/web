ARTS INTRODUCTION

What is Arts?

Arts is a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination.
The term art encompasses diverse media such as painting, sculpture, printmaking, drawing, decorative arts,
photography, and installation.

Types of Arts

Visual Arts – arts that we can see, like drawing, painting, and sculpture.

Performing Arts – arts that are performed in front of people, like dancing, singing, and acting.

Literary Arts – arts that use words, like poetry, stories, and plays
